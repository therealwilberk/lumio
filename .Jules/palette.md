## 2024-05-24 - [Signup Form Feedback & Accessibility]
**Learning:** Real-time feedback for multi-step validation (like PIN requirements and matching) significantly improves the user experience by reducing cognitive load during form completion. Pairing these with reactive mascot animations adds a layer of delight that reinforces positive behavior.
**Action:** When implementing forms with specific constraints, always provide a real-time checklist and ensure all interactive elements (like visibility toggles) have explicit ARIA labels and high-visibility focus states.
